About the App

Self Manager is designed to help individuals organize their daily tasks, manage personal goals, track habits, and improve overall productivity. The application focuses on simplicity, consistency, and actionable insights so users can take better control of their time and personal growth.

Key Features

- Task and goal management
- Habit tracking and reminders
- Personal progress monitoring
- Clean and distraction-free experience
