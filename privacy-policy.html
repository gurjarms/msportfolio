This Privacy Policy explains how the Self Manager mobile application collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

The app may collect the following information:

  • Basic account information when you sign in using Google
  • Attendance records entered by the user
  • Personal notes created within the app
  • Home expense records for family members
  • Backup files created by the user

Google Sign-In and Google Drive

Our app uses Google Sign-In to authenticate users. With your explicit permission, the app may access Google Drive to upload and download backup files created by you.

We do not access, read, or modify any files on your Google Drive other than the backup files generated by this application.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide attendance tracking functionality
  • To allow users to write and manage personal notes
  • To manage and calculate family home expenses
  • To create, restore, download, and share backup files
  • To improve app stability and user experience

Data Storage and Security

User data is stored locally on the device and/or in the user's Google Drive account as backup. We implement reasonable security practices to protect your information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Backup sharing occurs only when the user explicitly chooses to share files.

User Control

  • You can delete your data from the app at any time
  • You can revoke Google Drive access from your Google Account settings
  • You control when backups are uploaded or downloaded

Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
